Tasked with identifying a problem on campus and solving it in three months, my group was able to identify an issue troubling Cornell students. We interviewed and researched, made prototypes, conducted usability tests and ended with a report and presentation of our solution: Big Red Trade.

Why UX?


You might have noticed the hand-drawn elements scattered across this website: simply put, I love to create. My drawings on this website, including the embellishments, my logo, and many of the images, are my way of putting myself in my work.
